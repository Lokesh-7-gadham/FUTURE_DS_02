## Key Insights – Facebook Ad Campaign Analysis

• The campaign generated a high reach with approximately 79 million impressions.  

• Overall Click Through Rate (CTR) stands at 1.69%, indicating moderate user engagement.  

• The 30–34 age group recorded the highest number of clicks and ad spend.  

• Click activity peaked during the middle of the campaign period, suggesting optimal engagement days. 
 
• Cost Per Click (CPC) averaged $1.51, reflecting cost-efficient ad delivery.  

• Conversion rate remained low at 0.21%, indicating the campaign was more effective for awareness and traffic than conversions.
